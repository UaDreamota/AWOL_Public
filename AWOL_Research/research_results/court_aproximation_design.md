# Court aproximation design (WiP)

As we are interested in the geospatial and potentially institutional co-variates of latent AWOL, it is
crucial to know where each soldier was stationed before going AWOL. 

Unfortunately, there is no such data publicly available and we have to approximate it. The main source of
AWOL data is court decisions, as soldiers going AWOL are prosecuted and court decisions are public.
